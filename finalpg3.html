Frequently Asked Questions

Gharpayy is a trusted platform connecting you to premium rental accommodations—PGs, co-living spaces, and flats—primarily in Bangalore, with verified listings and seamless user support.

Our process is designed to be hassle-free. You can browse verified listings, schedule virtual or in-person visits, book your room online with a small token amount, and move in. We handle all the paperwork and provide 24/7 support after you move in.

We prioritize quality and trust. Unlike open listing platforms, every Gharpayy property is physically inspected and verified.

We believe in 100% transparency. All properties require a standard security deposit (usually one month's rent), which is clearly stated on the listing and is fully refundable. There are no hidden brokerage fees or surprise charges.

Most of our properties are fully furnished and include high-speed Wi-Fi, regular housekeeping, and 24/7 security. Specific amenities like in-house meals, laundry, or gym access are clearly listed on each property's detail page.

We've got it covered. Every tenant gets access to our support portal. If anything breaks—a lightbulb, a tap, or the Wi-Fi—simply raise a ticket, and our dedicated maintenance team will resolve it quickly at no extra cost to you.
