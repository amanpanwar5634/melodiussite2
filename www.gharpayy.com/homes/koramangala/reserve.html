FAQs

Here are all the answers to your questions about Homes Reserve.
What is Homes Reserve?

Reserve makes securing your Homes home simple and stress-free. Just complete a quick form to share your preferences, pay the booking fee, and we’ll take care of the rest. In just a few weeks, we’ll deliver a fully furnished, designer home tailored to your needs.

Does Homes guarantee finding me a home if I join Reserve?

While we strive to match every client with a home, Homes Reserve works on availability. We prioritize your preferences and work to find a suitable home within the specified timeline. However, in rare cases where we cannot fulfill your request, a full refund will be provided.

When & how will I get my refund?

If you decide not to continue at any point during the process, we will initiate a full refund within 24 hours of receiving your request. The funds will be returned to your original payment method within 5-7 working days.

Do I get a full refund even after I visit the house?

Yes, you are eligible for a full refund even if you’ve visited the house but decide not to proceed. Simply communicate your decision, and we will process the refund within 24 hours. The funds will be returned to your original payment method within 5-7 working days.

How do I initiate a refund if I don’t want to continue with Reserve?

To initiate a refund, simply reach out to us via email or phone, and we will process your request within 24 hours. The funds will be returned to your original payment method within 5-7 working days.

What if I move into a Homes home but don’t like it?

We strive to address all your queries before you move into a Homes home to ensure a comfortable stay. If you're uncertain about moving in, we recommend choosing a no-lock-in plan to give us a try. If you opt for a lock-in plan, please note that if you wish to exit before the lock-in period is over, your deposit will be forfeited.

How long does it take to get my home?

Once you’ve submitted your preferences and paid the booking fee, our team will begin curating your fully furnished, designer home. You can expect to receive your home within the timeframe specified during the booking process, typically within the booked slot date or week. If you have any specific requests or timelines, feel free to communicate them, and we’ll do our best to accommodate you.

What if I decide not to continue?

If you change your mind at any point during the process, just let us know. We’ll initiate a refund within 24 hours of receiving your request, and the funds will be returned to your original payment method within 5-7 working days.

Is the booking fee 100% refundable?

Yes, the booking fee is fully refundable. If you proceed with us, the amount will be adjusted against your first month's rent or deposit. If you decide not to go ahead, the funds will be returned to your original payment method within 5-7 working days after you notify us.

Can I choose the location of my home?

Yes, during the preference submission process, you’ll have the opportunity to specify your preferred locations. We will do our best to find a home in the area that suits your needs.

Is there a minimum stay requirement?

Yes, our no-lock-in plan requires a minimum stay of 30 days. However, the minimum stay may vary depending on the property and location. All relevant details will be provided upfront before you confirm your reservation.

What happens if there is a delay in finding my home?

We aim to meet our promised timeline, but if there’s an unexpected delay, we will keep you informed and work to resolve it as quickly as possible. If we cannot meet your needs in a reasonable timeframe, you may request a refund.

Are utilities included in the furnished home?

Utilities are generally not included unless specifically stated. However, the home comes fully furnished and equipped with essential appliances. Services like Wi-Fi, LPG connection, etc, are set up for your convenience, but the cost is to be borne by you (on actuals). Homes ensures that everything is ready before you move in.

Can I visit the home before I move in?

Yes, once the home has been curated & ready, you’ll have the opportunity to visit and ensure everything meets your expectations before moving in.

What if I need assistance after moving in?

Our support team is available to help with any issues or concerns you may have after moving in. Whether it’s a maintenance request or something else, we’re here to ensure your experience is smooth and hassle-free.

Do you offer pet-friendly homes?

Yes, we do offer pet-friendly homes. When you submit your preferences, be sure to indicate that you have a pet, and we’ll find a suitable home for you and your furry friend.
